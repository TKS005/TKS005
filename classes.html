MMA Classes

BJJ Classes

Our BJJ, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu, classes will be lead by UFC Featherweight Contender, Bryce Mitchell. Bryce holds the all time record for body twister submissions in all of MMA history (2). He currently holds the most submissions among active UFC featherweights.

Muay Thai Classes

Our Muay Thai classes will be led by current UFC Middleweight champion, Israel Addesanya. Addesanya currently holds the most title defenses in UFC Middleweight history (9).

Sparring Sessions

Our sparring sessions are held once a week on Thursdays at 11:00pm. We will have MMA Referee Bobby Wombacher as the referee for our sparring sessions.

In order to Participate in our Sparring Sessions, you must meet the following Criteria:

  • Successfully pass our BJJ AND Muay Thai Classes
  • Be medically cleared by our on-site MMA Doctor
  • Have a team to corner you
  • Will to fight and destroy your opponent!
